# 

## Hello BSides

**Category:** Getting Started  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Target Time:** 15

## Scenario

You need to get something printed onto the screen of the T-Dongle. That something can be a simple message or a fun image. USBArmyKnife is recommended as it is easier to do this with, but you can get creative.

THE DOCUMENTATION IS YOUR FRIEND.

**IMPORTANT NOTE**
You will need a spare SD card to get USBArmyKnife to boot. You can also create a custom firmware that displays on the screen.

You can also come to the station and connect to the `hello-bsides` with password `password` and run a command to change the content of the screen.

The URL is http://4.3.2.1:8080 to access the WebUI.

(Raw commands are in the command tab, running raw commands)

## Your Goal

Complete the lab by:

- Have something more than a black screen on the T-Dongle  
- Have a message or image that you made show up on the screen

## What You Can Use

- The LilyGo device and station materials  
- Your own laptop/phone, if allowed  
- AI, search engines, and documentation  
- Help from a volunteer if you get stuck

## Rules

- The message or image must be something other than the default black screen  
- You can get creative in what you have it say or show, just keep it appropriate  
- Have fun and learn

## Starting Point

1. Your T-Dongle with some sort of firmware on it  
2. Have your documentation ready  
3. Have a plan on your chosen firmware on how to accomplish it

## Checkpoints

You are probably on the right track if:

- You have a script written up  
- You found a way to upload the script to the T-Dongle

## Completion

To finish the station, show a volunteer:

- The screen of the T-Dongle with a message or image

**Need a hint? Ask a volunteer.**
